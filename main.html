Expert Advice & Tips

Professional insights to help you plan the perfect event, wedding, or travel experience.

Rajasthani Wedding Traditions

Experience unique Rajasthani customs like the 'Panihari' welcome ceremony, folk music performances, and traditional jewelry adorned with precious stones.

Palace Wedding Planning

Book palaces 12-18 months in advance. Consider seasonal weather - winter (Nov-Feb) for comfortable ceremonies, summer for fewer crowds.

Desert Wedding Logistics

Plan desert weddings during cooler months (Oct-Mar). Arrange transportation for guests, consider AC tents, and have backup indoor venues.

Royal Guest Experience

Ensure palace accommodations for all guests. Include traditional Rajasthani cuisine, cultural performances, and local sightseeing opportunities.

Rajasthan Travel Etiquette

Respect local customs, dress modestly in religious sites, bargain respectfully in markets, and always ask permission before photographing people.

Best Time to Visit Rajasthan

October to March offers pleasant weather for outdoor ceremonies. April-May brings desert festivals, June-September is monsoon season.
